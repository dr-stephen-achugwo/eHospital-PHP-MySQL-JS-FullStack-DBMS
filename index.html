About us


We are the team behind this innovative project. Our mission is to provide patients with an easy and convenient way to access medical services and information. With our e-hospital, patients can receive consultation and treatment online, monitor their health status and receive laboratory results without having to leave the comfort of their home.
We believe that the e-hospital is the future of medicine. With the help of technology, we can change the way people receive medical care and make them more free and independent.
